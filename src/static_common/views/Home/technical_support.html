Technical Support

If you are experiencing technical difficulties, please try the following steps:

  • Step 1:
    Make sure that you have normal internet access. If no, please contact your technical support team at your institution.
  • Step 2:
    Empty your browser cache to ensure the activation of any product updates. Please find a complete set of instructions for how to do so for all possible web browsers: http://www.refreshyourcache.com
  • Step 3:
    If the aforementioned steps are not working, please contact the account manager.
