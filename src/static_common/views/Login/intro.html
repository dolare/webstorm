Upgrid  - Website content review & consulting

Upgrid is a website-content review, research and consulting service offered by Gridology Education & Technology, Inc. (GET) to graduate institutions throughout the United States. Graduate program websites are one of the most important tools for attracting and recruiting prospective graduate students. The Upgrid service is designed to help you maximize the potential returns of an informative and easy-to-navigate website. This product ensures that your website content is kept flawless, while also keeping you up to date on the latest changes made by your competitors.
