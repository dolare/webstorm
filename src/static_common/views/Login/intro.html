2. Products for
EXECUTIVE AND CONTINUING EDUCATION

1) For institutions with Open-Enrollment Offerings (Programs for Individuals)

Stay on top of the latest changes that competitors are making to their category and course offerings, all while equipping yourself with specialized analytical tools that allow you to deepen your insight into underlying trends in the field.

Complete Report on Peers from past to present and beyond

  • Gain access to a complete set of your competitors’ category and course offerings with relevant data gathered automatically for you on an ongoing basis – keeping you up to date on the most recent happenings in your field.
  • Revealing and displaying the changes and updates made by your peers in an intuitive manner - keeping you informed of the latest strategic moves made by your peers.

Peer Analysis Tools - deepen your insight

  • Gain access to the past with historical snapshots of your peers, – allowing you to better analyze exactly why and how they have arrived at their present format.
  • Equip yourself with a side-by-side comparison tool that will deepen your analysis into the similarities and differences in your peers’ categories, courses, locations, course types, scheduling, tuition data, and more.
  • Dig deeper into competitors offerings with a specialized keyword search tool, enhancing your ability to analyze trends in competitors offerings directly and with ease.

2) For institutions with Customized Offerings (Programs for Organizations)

Deepen your access to and understanding of peer’s marketing strategies for and actions taken with their customized programs via an ongoing assessment and evaluation algorithm providing insights into both your own and your peers’ marketing efforts.
